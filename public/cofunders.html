Ambassadors & Co-funders

Late 2016, a group of passionate entrepreneurs had a brilliant idea: create a place where digital could become accessible to everyone. hey needed to find a place to make their dream come true, a place where people could meet, share and learn. They wanted a central place. And guess what? They found the absolute must: Brussels Central Station. With more than 700.000 travellers passing through the station every week, the location was ideal to develop their mission. Our cofounders and ambassadors brought BeCentral to life. Nothing would have bene possible without them and we are incredibly proud to have them on board!
