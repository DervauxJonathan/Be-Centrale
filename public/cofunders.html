Our Co-funders

Late 2016, a group of passionate entrepreneurs had an idea: create a central hub where digital could become accessible to everyone. And guess what? They found the absolute must: Brussels Central Station. With more than 60.000 travellers passing through the station every day, the location was ideal to develop their mission. Nothing would have been possible without them!
