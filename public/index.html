01 About

Ticket To Digital

Ticket To Digital

BeCentral is a new digital campus located in Brussels Central Station. Cofounded & backed-up by more than 40 entrepreneurs, we’re on a mission to close the digital skills gap and help to accelerate Belgium’s Digital Transformation. How do we do this ? By hosting digital schools or workshops in an entrepreneurial ecosystem. Check out our fantastic 30+ initiatives, our next events and available workshops below!

02 Co-founders

Co-Founder & Amabassadors

Late 2016, a group of passionate entrepreneurs had an idea: create a central hub where digital could become accessible to everyone. And guess what? They found the absolute must: Brussels Central Station. With more than 60.000 travellers passing through the station every day, the location was ideal to develop their mission. Nothing would have been possible without them!

You can find us in the heart of Brussels, on top of the Central Station.important note As our entry doors are kept closed for security reasons, it is best to call the person you are visiting. Make sure to have his or her telephone number at hand. In case you don’t have it, you can call +32 488 45 42 62.


in case you arrive by train

  • Go to the main hall of the Brussels Central Station, take the stairs next to/to the left hand side of the Starbucks café.
  • Leave the station to arrive on rue Cantersteen.
  • Stay on this side of the road.
  • Go to the left.
  • You’ll find us at number 10.


in case you arrive by car Put in Cantersteen 10 or 12 in your GPS system. Nearest parking spaces are Interparking Albertine-Square or Interparking Grand Place.


BeCentral Reception You can find us on the 2nd floor which is accessible via the elevator or the stairs on the right hand side of the elevator. The stairs in front of the entrance door go up to the 1st floor only. Once you are on the 2nd floor, follow the sign ‘The Factory/ BeCentral team’. See you soon.
