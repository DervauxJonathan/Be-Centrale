01 About

Ticket To Digital

Ticket To Digital

BeCentral is a vibrant Digital Campus located on top of Brussels Central Station.


Co-founded by a group of passionate entrepreneurs, our mission of closing the digital skills gap and accelerate Belgium’s digital transformation came to life in October 2017.

Our awesome community of 25+ digital initiatives – or 200+ residents on campus every day – contribute to our mission by exchanging their insights and experiences.

Since October, we welcomed 100+ events to bring digital closer to people (meetups, inspirational or educational talks) or to companies (trainings, workshops, seminars, bootcamps).We’re on a mission to bring digital close to you. So, are you ready to jump onboard?

Our Co-Founder & Amabassadors

Late 2016, 28 passionate members of the Belgium's tech community got an idea: create a new digital campus that would be as accessible as possible. They needed to find a place where Digital Transformation would be more approchable, a place where people could share, meet and connect.
